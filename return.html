At Swiss, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, we are here to help with our Returns and Exchanges policy. Please read the following information carefully to ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience. Returns We accept returns within 30 days of the original purchase date. To be eligible for a return, the item must be in its original condition, unused, and in the same packaging that you received it. Additionally, you must have a proof of purchase, such as an order confirmation or receipt. How to Initiate a Return Log in to your account on our website. Go to the “Order History” section. Find the order containing the item you want to return and click on it. Select the item(s) you wish to return and follow the prompts to initiate the return process. Return Shipping Customers are responsible for return shipping costs, except in cases where the return is due to an error on our part. We recommend using a trackable shipping service to ensure the safe return of your item. Refunds Once we receive your return, we will inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund. If your return is approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will be automatically applied to your original method of payment. Please allow up to 5 business days for the refund to appear in your account. Exchanges If you received a damaged or defective item and need to exchange it for the same item, please contact our customer support team within 14 days of receiving the product. We will guide you through the exchange process. Returning Gifts If you received an item as a gift and would like to return or exchange it, please contact our customer support team. We will assist you in processing the return or exchange, even if you don’t have the original proof of purchase. Non-Returnable Items Please note that the following items are non-returnable: Final Sale Items: Items marked as “Final Sale” are not eligible for return.
